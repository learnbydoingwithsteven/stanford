# Stanford CS Complete Course Index

**Comprehensive catalog of all CS courses by number**

---

## 📚 Course Numbering System

- **CS 100-199**: Undergraduate courses
- **CS 200-299**: Graduate courses (MS/PhD)
- **CS 300-399**: Advanced graduate seminars and research
- **CS 400+**: Special topics and independent study

## 🎯 Course Selection Tips

### For Undergraduates
1. **Start with Core**: Complete CS106A/B, CS107, CS103, CS109, CS110, CS161
2. **Choose Track**: Select AI, Systems, Theory, HCI, or Graphics
3. **Balance Load**: Mix heavy and light courses
4. **Prerequisites**: Plan ahead for prerequisite chains
5. **Electives**: Take courses outside CS for breadth

### For Master's Students
1. **Satisfy Foundations**: Ensure foundation requirements met
2. **Complete Breadth**: Take 3 courses from different areas
3. **Focus Depth**: Choose specialization and take 21+ units
4. **Research Early**: Start research in first year if interested
5. **Plan Ahead**: Map out 3-4 quarter plan

### For PhD Students
1. **Front-Load Coursework**: Take most courses in years 1-2
2. **Breadth First**: Cover breadth requirements early
3. **Depth as Needed**: Take depth courses relevant to research
4. **Audit Later**: Audit courses after requirements met
5. **Research Focus**: Prioritize research after year 2
